# HR Analytics Dashboard

## Overview

This project analyzes employee attrition trends using HR data and provides actionable insights through an interactive Power BI dashboard. The objective is to identify factors influencing employee turnover and support data-driven workforce retention strategies.

## Business Problem

Employee attrition can lead to increased recruitment costs, productivity loss, and reduced organizational efficiency. Understanding the drivers of attrition helps organizations improve employee retention and workforce planning.

## Dashboard KPIs

* Total Employees: 1470
* Total Attrition: 237
* Attrition Rate: 16.1%
* Average Salary: $6.5K

## Features

* Attrition Analysis by Department
* Attrition Analysis by Job Role
* Attrition Analysis by Age Group
* Attrition Analysis by Salary Slab
* Attrition Analysis by Years at Company
* Gender-wise Attrition Analysis
* Interactive Filtering by Department, Job Role, Gender, and Marital Status

## Tools Used

* Power BI
* Power Query
* DAX
* SQL
* Data Visualization

## Key Insights

* Employees aged 18–25 show the highest attrition rate.
* Employees earning up to $5K exhibit significantly higher turnover.
* Sales department experiences the highest attrition rate.
* Sales Representatives and Laboratory Technicians are among the most affected job roles.
* Attrition is highest during the early years of employment.

## Business Impact

The dashboard enables HR teams to identify high-risk employee groups, improve retention initiatives, and support workforce planning through data-driven insights.
